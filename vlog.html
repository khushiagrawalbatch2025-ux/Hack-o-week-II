Welcome to Paris

Paris is one of the most beautiful cities in the world. Known for romance, art, culture, and fashion, the city offers breathtaking architecture, charming streets, delicious pastries, and unforgettable experiences.

The Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is the symbol of Paris. Built in 1889, it attracts millions of visitors every year. At night, the tower sparkles with thousands of lights creating a magical view.

The Louvre Museum

The Louvre Museum is the largest museum in the world and home to famous artworks including the Mona Lisa. Its glass pyramid entrance is one of the most recognizable modern structures in Paris.
